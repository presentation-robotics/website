Presentation Invasion Robotics Team 2135

Our Mission

The young women of the Presentation Invasion Team 2135 have come together with our various talents, ideas, and backgrounds to take on the 2011 FIRST Robotics challenge. We will be respectful to each other and to the other teams we meet. We promise to try our best at all times and succeed in the completion of our goals.

"This is not just a team, it is a family."

News

Presentation Invasion Featured in Wired Magazine

January 8, 2013

On Saturday a reporter from Wired Magazine came to the shop and interviewed a few of the members about Kickoff. Today the article he wrote about FIRST and our team was published. The article can be found here.

American Association for University Women Event

September 10, 2011

The Presentation Robotics Team was invited to give a presentation of our robots at the September meeting of the San Jose branch of the American Association for University Women. We had a great time talking about our work and our plans for the upcoming competition season!

Bag and Tag Day

March 3, 2011

Instead of shipping our robot to competition this year, we were required to "bag and tag" the robot and transport it to SVR ourselves. This year, Bag and Tag Day was on February 22. In the "bag and tag" system, the robot is placed in a bag and labeled with a tag labeled with a certain number. We had a fun time bagging up our robot, and can't wait to unbag it at SVR!
