Presentation Invasion Robotics Team 2135

Our Mission

The young women of the Presentation Invasion Team 2135 have come together with our various talents, ideas, and backgrounds to take on the 2011 FIRST Robotics challenge. We will be respectful to each other and to the other teams we meet. We promise to try our best at all times and succeed in the completion of our goals.

"This is not just a team, it is a family."

News

Silicon Valley Regional Competition

March 30, 2012



Though we didn't make it into the elimination rounds, our team still had a lot of fun at the Silicon Valley Regional Competition. This concludes the 2012 season. We look forward to competing next year!

Sacramento - UC Davis Competition

March 17, 2012



Our team took sixth place at the Davis Competition in Sacramento. Go Presentation Invasion!

Build Season

February 11, 2012



Our team has been hard at work constructing the robot for this year's competition season!

Team Bonding

December 10, 2011

We took a break from our hard work by having a team bonding party at Golfland! We all had a lot of fun.

Madera Competition

November 11-12, 2011

The Presentation Robotics Team competed at the 2011 Madera Competition and finished as semifinalists. Go Presentation Invasion!

Open House

November 6, 2011

The team demonstrated our robots and discussed our upcoming plans at the 2011 Presentation Open House.

CalGames

October 21-22, 2011

Though we didn't compete, the Presentation Robotics Team volunteered at the 2011 CalGames Tournament. It was a great experience and we had a lot of fun!

American Association for University Women Event

September 10, 2011

The Presentation Robotics Team was invited to give a presentation of our robots at the September meeting of the San Jose branch of the American Association for University Women. We had a great time talking about our work and our plans for the upcoming competition season!

Bag and Tag Day

March 3, 2011

Instead of shipping our robot to competition this year, we were required to "bag and tag" the robot and transport it to SVR ourselves. This year, Bag and Tag Day was on February 22. In the "bag and tag" system, the robot is placed in a bag and labeled with a tag labeled with a certain number. We had a fun time bagging up our robot, and can't wait to unbag it at SVR!
