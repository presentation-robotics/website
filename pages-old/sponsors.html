Sponsors

Because of all these companies and individuals who have devoted time, money and parts to help us, we have been able to be as successful as we are right now.

Presentation High School

Founded in 1962, Presentation High School is a college-preparatory high school for girls whose fundamental beliefs are derived from the Catholic Church and the educational ministry of the Sisters of the Presentation. The school strives to combine education with the Catholic vision found in the Gospels. Each student is challenged to become a woman of faith, dedicated to working with others, intellectually competent and committed to her personal growth. Presentation works in partnership with the family, which is the primary educator. Presentation assumes responsibility for using its unique resources to bring about its stated purposes. Presentation recognizes the following principles as the foundation upon which all our goals, outcomes, programs and procedures are developed and evaluated. Led by Ms. Mary Miller, our principal, Presentation High School provides the teamÅfs meeting and workshop facilities at the Jenvey House across the street. Thank you to Presentation for providing the Jenvey House!

Google

Google has kindly sponsored us since we first began the team back in 2007. We would not be as successful as we are today without their consistent financial support. Thank you Google for your continual financial aid as we continue to grow as a team.

Exatron

It is Exatron's philosophy to provide well-made and reasonably priced equipment designed to match our varying needs. They are manufacturers of a complete line of automated handling, testing, programming, and marking equipment for the package integrated circuit industry. T hey provided support and guidance in the robot build process. Currently, they have helped us immensely with our chassis for the 2011 season. Mr Robert Howell, President of Exatron, is a member of the PHS Math & Science Academy Advisory Council. Thanks Exatron for machining some parts for us, including the chassis.

Video Propulsion

VideoPropulsion Interactive Television, Inc. has been a world leader in hardware and software for high performance and low cost per stream digital content movement. The company has established a reputation for providing unique High Definition Television (HDTV), Video on Demand (VoD), and Internet Protocol Television (IPTV) products. Thank you to Video Propulsion for giving consistent guidance to the team whenever necessary.

SolidWorks

SolidWorks is a cadding program that helps create three dimensional images and a plan of our robot and helps us visualize how the robot will look. Thank you Solidworks for providing free licenses for us to use and experiment with while constructing and designing our robot.
